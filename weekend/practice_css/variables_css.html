Welcome to TechHub


Your Coding Journey Starts Here


TechHub is the leading coding community at National Post Graduate College. We empower students to learn, build, and innovate through hands-on projects and collaborative learning. Join us today and transform your passion for technology into real-world skills.


About TechHub Coding Club


Founded in 2018, TechHub has grown into a vibrant community of over 200 active members. We believe in learning by doing and have successfully organized 50+ workshops, hackathons, and tech talks. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by providing practical coding experience and mentorship.


Our Vision


To create a community where every student, regardless of their background, can explore technology, develop their skills, and build innovative solutions that make a difference.
