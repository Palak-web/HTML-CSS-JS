Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients List:

  • 200g Paneer (cubed)
  • 2 medium Onion(chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 3 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander leaves (for garnish)

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan.
  2. Saut√© onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Mix in red chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  6. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Stir in fresh cream and simmer for 2 minutes.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Cooking Time Table:

Time Type Duration
Prep Time 15 mins
Cook Time 20 mins
Total Time 35 mins
